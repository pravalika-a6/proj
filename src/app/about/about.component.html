About Us


MYSOCIETY app makes place secure and more convenient for residents. The mysociety app was built as a comprehensive society management System keeping in mind every single stakeholder of a gated community or apartment


The benefits provided by mysociety are:

1. Improved Convenience: Manage Daily Help(maid,cook,electrician,plumber,etc)

2. UseDigital Communication : You can read your notice board,raise complaints,find contact details of committee members,your neighbours,and get emergency Contacts

3. Enjoy easy bookkeeping: The society management can maintain resident details which makes it easier for the committee to manage.
